About Our Conference

Welcome to Franciscan Sister's School's inaugural Model United Nations conference! Join us for an exciting event where students will engage in diplomatic discussions, problem-solving, and resolution writing while representing countries from around the world.

Committees

UN Women

United Nations Entity for Gender Equality and the Empowerment of Women
Focused on women's rights, equality, and empowerment. Delegates will debate gender-based challenges and global solutions.

UNHRC

United Nations Human Rights Council
Addresses pressing human rights issues around the world. Delegates will work to uphold and protect global human rights.

DISEC

Disarmament and International Security Committee
Discusses international peace and security, focusing on arms control and disarmament.

UNHSC

United Nations (Historical) Security Council
Topic: The 2003 Invasion of Iraq
A simulation of the Security Council in 2003. Delegates revisit the debates and decisions surrounding the Iraq War.

Crisis Committee

The World on Edge: Operation Black Dawn
A fictional near-future scenario where a rogue nation develops an AI-guided hypersonic missile. Delegates must navigate a high-stakes global crisis in real-time.
