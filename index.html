About Our Conference

Welcome to Franciscan Sister's School's inaugural Model United Nations conference! Join us for an exciting event where students will engage in diplomatic discussions, problem-solving, and resolution writing while representing countries from around the world.

Committees

Committee details will be announced soon. Our conference will include beginner-friendly committees as well as some for more experienced delegates.

HSC - Historical Security Council

Step back in time with our Historical Security Council committee, where delegates will tackle critical international security challenges from the past. Participants will engage with historical crises and conflicts, making diplomatic decisions that could alter the course of simulated history.
